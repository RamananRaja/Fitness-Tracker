Hair & Skin
🎯 Goal: Eliminate dandruff + healthy scalp. Your smoothie already contains the top 4 anti-dandruff nutrients internally. Combine with the routine below.
Why Dandruff Happens & How to Fix It
🦠
Malassezia Fungus
Most common cause. Use Nizoral (ketoconazole) shampoo 2×/week for first month. Available at any Chennai medical shop — no prescription.
💧
Dry Scalp
Chennai heat dehydrates scalp. Coconut oil + neem oil massage 1 hr before wash. Your 3L water daily helps from inside.
🥗
Zinc Deficiency
Cashews in your smoothie directly supply zinc — the #1 mineral studied for dandruff reduction. Expect improvement in 3–4 weeks.
🌶️
Scalp Inflammation
Walnuts (Omega-3) in your smoothie reduce inflammation at the follicle level. Works from inside out.
What Your Smoothie Already Does for Your Scalp
